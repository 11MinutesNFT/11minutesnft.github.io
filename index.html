ARE YOU READY TO CHANGE YOUR LIFE
IN JUST 11 MINUTES?
11Minutes is a recurring game event that gives you the opportunity to change your entire life within 11 minutes.
Each game has its own participant NFT token. Owners of these tokens have the chance to take part in the game and win your main prize. A total of up to 75 ETH will be distributed in the first game. The price increases with every new game.
Rules
  • In order to be able to participate in the game, you must have an NFT of the respective game session in possession
  • As soon as the start time of a session is announced, you can start the game within 24 hours. You have to prove your skills within 11 minutes of starting the game
  • Within the 11 minutes of playing time you have as many attempts as you want to achieve a high score. Your personal high score will then be entered in the leaderboard
  • Owners of several NFTs can start the game once per NFT and each have 11 minutes.
  • If you do not start the game within 24 hours you will lose your chance to win a prize.
  • The NFT's that you have purchased will not be burned and will remain in your possession
  • The prizes are determined by position in the leaderboard. The levels are as follows: Top 25%, Top 10%, Top 5%, Top 1% and of course first place on the leasderboard.
  • In addition to cash prizes, everyone who lands in the top 25% will win an NFT for the next game session
FAQ
How can I participate in the games?
Mint one or more tokensas soon as minting starts here!
How do I get notified of new games?
All games will be announced on the website, discord, and twitter. So stay tuned.
Does owning more tokens increase my chances to win the prize?
Yes, the more players your own, the more chances to break the high score you have.
How many players will share the prize?
The prize money will be distributed depending on the position on the leader board. We differentiate between the top 25%, top 10%, top 5%, top 1% and of course first place.
How do the games work? How many games will there be?
No details about the games are disclosed. You have the opportunity to start the game once per NFT you have within 24 hours and to achieve a high score within 11 minutes.
What information is available about the games?
None of the games will be games of chance.
Is my NFT going to get burned if I'm not in the Top 25%?
No, it will never be burned. Each NFT is a trophy that you can be proud of and that you can keep forever.
What if I am at work or sleeping?
We assume that within 24 hours everyone can find the opportunity to change their life within 11 minutes.
What if I miss the game?
If you miss a game, you cannot get a prize.
How is the prize pool funded, where is it stored?
60% of primary sales have been used to fund the prize. It is stored in the smart contract for everyone to see.
Why do you use the polygon sidechain?
Polygon is a full-fledged sidechain from Ethereum that is perfect for games and is fully supported by OpenSea.
